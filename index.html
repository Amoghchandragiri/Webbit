About Us

Our Coding Club dates back to 2012 when it first started. It was started as an initiative led by 3rd year Seniors at that time to help their juniors in acquiring skills and getting to know about the various talents and skills which they should possess before graduation.

The Club's main intention was to enlighten juniors about the competitiveness of the job market and the cutting-edge technologies on which famous tech companies are working on. In this club, Seniors help juniors learn technologies and frameworks outside of their syllabus and be enthusiastic to learn more about them.Below are the various fields our Club members try to educate about our juniors upon.

Fields

UI/UX

UI[User Interface] is a way through which an user interacts with an application or a website. A UI designer's job is to create an attractive and visually stimulating interface for an application or website. UX[User Experience] is a process used to create applications or websites which provide a relevant and meaningful experiences to the users.

Web/App Development

Web Development is process of creating, maintaining and deploying websites. It is a creation of an application that works on Internet. App Development is a process of writing software that works on a mobile device. It is a strategic process of creating, launching a successful mobile product.

Competitive Coding

Competitive coding is the art of coding a problem under limited amount of time. It enables you to think of strategies of finding innovative solutions under limited amount of time.
